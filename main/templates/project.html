COSMETIC BRAND SOCIAL MEDIA POST PERFORMANCE DASHBOARD

This cosmetic brand utilized four content types throughout the year: photos, videos, status updates, and links. Content performance analysis reveals that link posts generate high reach with low engagement, while photo content achieves superior engagement rates. Temporal optimization shows Sunday as the optimal posting day due to peak engagement rates. Posting schedule analysis identifies 5 AM and evening hours as the most effective times for content distribution, maximizing audience interaction and overall campaign performance.

Lifestyle and Obesity Risk Analysis: Understanding how daily habits influence obesity levels

Based on 20,758 respondents, the data reveals significant diabetes risk patterns. Gender distribution shows Type 3 diabetes predominantly affects males while Type 2 primarily occurs in females. Smoking behavior strongly correlates with increased diabetes risk across all participants. Family history serves as a protective factor, with individuals lacking hereditary diabetes history showing substantially lower risk. Age analysis indicates diabetes onset peaks between 35-40 years.

Introvert vs Extrovert: Behavioral Patterns & Social Preferences

This dashboard reveals key behavioral differences between introverts and extroverts. Introverts need 8-10 hours of daily solitude versus extroverts' 2-4 hours, while extroverts maintain 3x larger social circles. The data shows extroverts attend events consistently while introverts are selective, with reversed social stamina patterns where introverts drain faster in groups while extroverts thrive longer.

FedHGN untuk Deteksi Kejahatan Transaksi dengan Kolaborasi antar Institusi Keuangan tanpa Melanggar Privasi Data

The FedHGN (Federated Heterogeneous Graph Network) enables financial crime detection through inter-institutional collaboration while preserving data privacy. Experimental results on the IBM AML dataset demonstrate an average accuracy of 97.19% with a macro F1-score of 49.29%, showcasing the model's effectiveness in detecting fraudulent transactions across federated financial institutions without compromising sensitive customer data.

ECG Clinical Classifier

This project proposes a novel multi-branch deep learning architecture for clinical ECG classification, combining a 1D ResNet for rhythm analysis, a 2D CNN for local morphological feature extraction, and another 2D CNN for global ECG interpretation. Evaluated on the PTB-XL dataset, the model achieves a macro AUC of 94.24% and an F-max score of 75.73%, demonstrating state-of-the-art performance in automated ECG interpretation.

FedHGN-Layering-Detection

FedHGN-Layering-Detection is a federated learning system that enables multiple banks to collaboratively detect money laundering without sharing sensitive transaction data. The framework uses heterogeneous graph neural networks to model financial relationships and employs RGCN to identify layering patterns while preserving data privacy through federated training approaches.

Coppa Risk Detection

The system analyzes mobile app metadata including developer country, ad spending, user ratings, privacy policy links, and security scores using six ML algorithms (Logistic Regression, Decision Tree, Random Forest, Gradient Boosting, XGBoost, and LightGBM). With comprehensive preprocessing and class imbalance handling, the project outputs AUC-ROC performance comparisons and probability predictions to help app stores and developers ensure compliance with child protection regulations.
